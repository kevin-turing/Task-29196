    Instructions

    • Click the dice to roll and start the game.
    • Select a piece to move by clicking on it.
    • Click on the board to move the selected piece.
    • A piece can only move if the dice value is 6 or if it has already started.
    • If a piece lands on a cell occupied by an opponent's piece, the opponent's piece is captured and returns to the starting position.
    • The first player to move all their pieces to the home column wins the game.
